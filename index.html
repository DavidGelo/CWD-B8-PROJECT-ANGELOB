PREAMBLE

We, chemical engineering undergraduate students, members of the Association of Chemical Engineering Students – Malayan Colleges Laguna , in order to form an organization that shall endeavor to promote camaraderie and cooperation among Chemical Engineering students, to be responsive to the needs of the community, to be excellent in academic and professional career, to be dedicated to the ideals of a responsible and innovative field in Engineering, to uphold equality and to respect individuality, imploring the aid of Almighty God, do hereby promulgate this constitution.


Section I Name

Section I Name

The organization shall be known as the Association of Chemical Engineering Students – Malayan Colleges Laguna, which for short shall be known as ACES – MCL. It shall be the official Chemical Engineering students’ organization of Malayan Colleges Laguna and a member/chapter of Philippine Institute of Chemical Engineers – Junior Chapter Luzon.

Section II Organization Symbol

Section II Organization Symbol

ACES – MCL shall adopt the official emblem which is composed of the name of the organization which stands for Association of Chemical Engineering Students of Malayan Colleges Laguna and an atom at the top. The atom represents the ACES organization as the building blocks of the CHE community, therefore promoting unity and camaraderie.

Article II Purpose

Section I Mission

ACES – MCL objectives shall be: 1. To uphold the camaraderie and cooperation between fellow chemical engineering students. 2. Educate members and instill awareness of the current happenings in preparation of becoming future problem solvers of the society worldwide. 3. To instill into each member mind the innate responsibility of a Chemical Engineer, that is to apply, abide, and be guided by the concept of sustainable development. 4. Encourage the members to become active participants in Community Services. 5. To involve the members in various activities and projects that will develop their academic performance.

fingerprint
Section II Vision

Section II Vision

ACES – MCL shall be the training grounds for future chemical engineers in application of the knowledge and technical skills acquired. It envisages its members to be flexible and well-formed guided by the Malayan Colleges Laguna Core Values.
